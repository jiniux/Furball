Most ConVars and ConFuncs will be prefixed with a 2-3 letter prefix, which is how most ConVars/Funcs are named
Here are the prefixes and their meanings:

cl  : Client (anything to do with the client, stuff like screen resolution etc)
sl  : Sound Library (anything to do with audio)
gl  : Graphics Library (anything to do with graphics)
net : Networking
cmr : Content Manager
sv  : Server
im  : Input Manager
nt  : Native Function (Stuff like nt_begin_run nt_on_exiting, they get called when the Game BeginRun() or OnExiting() Methods get called)
